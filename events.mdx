At Frontier Virtual Airlines, events are where our community truly comes alive. Our Events program is designed to bring pilots together through coordinated group flights, focus airports, limite-time challenges, seasonal events, etc.

## Our current events

**March Focus Airport - Palm Beach, Florida (KPBI)**

![KPBI](/images/KPBI.png)

Located in sunny **West Palm Beach, Florida**, Palm Beach is known for its luxury lifestyle, pristine beaches and historic charm. Located along Florida's Atlantic coastline, the area blends tropical beauty with elegance, attracting visitors from around the world. From it's stunning beaches, to historic architecture and arts and culture, Palm Beach is the place to be!

**March Focus Airport - San Francisco, California (KSFO)**

![KSFO](/images/KSFO.png)

Located in vibrant **San Francisco, California**, San Francisco is known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and breathtaking waterfront views. Situated along the Pacific coastline, the city blends historic character with modern innovation, attracting visitors from around the world. From its rolling hills and world-famous Golden-Gate Bridge, to rich arts, cuisine and cultural districts, SFO is truly one of America's most unforgettable destinations.

**March Focus Airport - Punta Cana, Dominican Republic (MDPC)**

![MDPC](/images/MDPC.png)

Located in beautiful **Punta Cana, Dominican Republic**, Punta Canada is known for its turquoise waters, white-sand beaches and laid-back tropical atmosphere. Situates along the eastern coast of the island, the area blends Carribean beauty with world-class resorts, attracting visitors from around the globe. From its stunning beachfronts and crystal-clear waters to vibrant culture and island hospitality, Punta Cana is a true paradise destination.
