title: "Accepted Aircrafts"
---

The **Accepted Aircrafts** Policy ensures operational consistency, realism and authenticity across Frontier Virtual Airlines. By limiting aircraft types to those operated by our real-world counterpart, we maintain realistic fleet operations, accurate assignments, consistent pilot experience and professional VA immersion.

<Note>
  Keep in mind that you are required to fly the aircraft that you booked, you **cannot** substitute. This means that if you book an Airbus A320neo, you cannot fly an A320-200 and vice-versa.
</Note>
