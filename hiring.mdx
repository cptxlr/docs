  <Accordion title="Liaison Team - VATSIM Liaison Coordinator">
    ### **Position Overview**

    Frontier Virtual Airlines is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented **VATSIM Liaison Coordinator** to lead our efforts in becoming a recognized Partner VA with VATSIM (Virtual Air Traffic Simulation Network). This role serves as the primary point of contact between our Virtual Airline and VATSIM leadership and audit staff.

    ### **Key Responsibilities**

    - Develop and implement a structured plan to obtain **VATSIM Partner VA** status.
    - Serve as the official liaison between Frontier Virtual Airlines and VATSIM.
    - Ensure all communications from VATSIM are acknowledged and responded to within **24 hours**.
    - Conduct internal compliance audits to ensure ongoing adherence to VATSIM policies and Partner VA requirements.
    - Coordinate and facilitate official audits conducted by VATSIM Audit Staff.
    - Provide leadership with regular updates on compliance status, audit findings, and corrective actions.
    - Recommend and implement policy or procedural adjustments as needed to maintain good standing.

    ### **Qualifications**

    - Strong knowledge of VATSIM policies, procedures, and Partner VA requirements.
    - Prior experience with Virtual Airlines or VATSIM operations preferred.
    - Excellent written communication skills.
    - Strong organizational and time management skills.
    - Ability to work independently and meet strict response deadlines.
    - Professional demeanor when representing the organization.

    ### **Preferred Attributes**

    - Previous staff or administrative experience within a Virtual Airline.
    - Experience participating in or facilitating compliance audits.
    - Familiarity with VATSIM Code of Conduct and Code of Regulations.

  <Accordion title="Liaison Team - Support Team Agent">
    ### **Position Overview**

    Frontier Virtual Airlines is seeking a motivated and service-oriented **Support Team Agent** to assist in providing timely and professional support to our pilots and staff. Under the supervision of the Liaison Team – Coordinator, this person is responsible for responding to emails and tickets in the helpdesk. They liaise between different departments and pilots, assisting pilots with requests and ensuring all inquiries are handled efficiently and professionally.

    ### **Key Responsibilities**

    - Respond to helpdesk emails and support tickets in a timely and professional manner.
    - Serve as a liaison between pilots and internal departments.
    - Assist pilots with questions, requests, and operational concerns to the best of your ability.
    - Escalate complex or unresolved issues to the Liaison Team – Coordinator when necessary.
    - Maintain accurate records of support interactions within the helpdesk system.
    - Ensure clear, courteous, and solution-focused communication at all times.
    - Identify recurring issues and recommend improvements where appropriate.

    ### **Qualifications**

    - Strong written communication skills.
    - Ability to manage multiple requests and prioritize effectively.
    - Strong organizational and time management skills.
    - Ability to work independently under supervision.
    - Professional demeanor when representing the organization.

    ### **Preferred Attributes**

    - Previous experience in customer service or helpdesk support.
    - Prior involvement with a Virtual Airline.
    - Experience working with internal teams or cross-department communication.
