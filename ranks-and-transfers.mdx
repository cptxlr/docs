# Rank Transfers

In order to maintain a level of competitivity with other airlines, we have decided to establish a Rank Transfers Policy.

<Warning>
  It is up to the person requesting the Rank Transfers to provide sufficient evidence to justify their transfers. For more information, please contact us at [help@frontier-va.com](mailto:help@frontier-va.com).
</Warning>

## Members of a current Frontier VA

If you are actively a member of a **current** Frontier Virtual Airline, the following applies to you:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Flight Hours">
    We will transfer over your total flight hours within that VA, plus an additional 10 Hours.Â 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PIREP Points">
    We will transfer over your total PIREP points within that VA, plus an additional 1,500 points.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pilot Rank">
    We will match your current rank within that VA, except for Staff roles which will obtain the equivalent rank to their Flight Hours and Points.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Members of defunct Frontier VA (Airways Alliance)

If you are a past member of Airways Alliance Frontier Virtual Airline, the following applies to you:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Flight Hours">
    - **If you have a proof of your flight hours**:
      - We will match the total flight hours from that VA.
    - **If you do not have a proof of your flight hours**:
      - Provide us with an approximate number of flight hours, we will evaluate on a case-by-case basis.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PIREP Points">
    There will not be any PIREP points given.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pilot Rank">
    Pilots Rank will depend on the amount of Flight Hours that we have recognized. They can be promoted up to Senior First Officer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Members of a different Virtual Airline (vAMSYS)

If you are a member of any other Virtual Airline that operated of vAMSYS.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Flight Hours">
    We will recognize your total amount of flight hours within that VA up to 100 Hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PIREP Points">
    There will not be any PIREP points given.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pilot Rank">
    Pilots Rank will depend on the amount of Flight Hours that we have recognized. They can be promoted up to First Officer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to apply for a Rank Transfer?

All rank transfer requests must be submitted either via our Email or by opening a ticket on the Helpdesk. Make sure to put in all the information and evidence in your request. Our Staff will review your application and contact you within 72 hours.

<Note>
  Make sure to provide all the information, failure to do so may result in additional delays.
</Note>
